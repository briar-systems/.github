# Code of Conduct

Briar Systems projects are built and discussed in good faith. We expect everyone who takes part to be respectful, constructive, and welcoming, and to assume the same of others.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, personal attacks, and anything that makes a project a worse place to take part in. Maintainers may edit or remove contributions, and may restrict participation, when this standard is not met.

To report a concern, email **security@briar.systems**. Reports are handled privately.

This states the spirit we hold each other to. A project may adopt the full [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org) where a formal policy is warranted.
